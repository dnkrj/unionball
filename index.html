Food

Our sumptuous three course dinner will tempt the most discerning tastebuds; a timeless culinary experience featuring the finest foods Cambridge has to offer. Or wander the ball and enjoy a wide variety of refreshments to sustain you through the ages. Discover our range of tantalising treats, from traditional classics such as candy floss and the succulent hog roast, to more adventurous and exciting flavours. With something for everyone our mouth watering snacks will keep you dancing until dawn!

Drink

The only history we won’t be celebrating at the bicentenary is that of prohibition. Let the Union take you on a whiskey-stop tour of the greatest beverages of the past 200 years. A champagne reception awaits you as you begin your journey. You’ll pass through a variety of cocktails, beers and the 1980s favourite alcopops as an accompaniment to your night in history. One thing that you can be sure of is that the drinks, both alcoholic and non, will be flowing throughout the night.
