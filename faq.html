Frequently Asked Questions

When do tickets go on sale?

Tickets go on sale to Union Members from 6pm -12pm on Thursday, 6th November.

Tickets go on sale to the rest of the University at 1:00pm on Friday, 7th November.

How much are the tickets?

Union members- £95 (dining) and £55 (non-dining)

University Members’ and all guests’ (including those of Union Members)- £105 (dining) and £65 (non-dining)

QueueJump tickets will cost an extra £10 on top of the standard ticket prices. QueueJump only applies to non-dining tickets.

What is QueueJump?

QueueJump tickets will allow you to enter The Bicentenary Ball earlier than normal tickets and will cost an extra £10.

This means less time spent waiting in the long queue and first access to food, drink and ents.

QueueJump only applies to non-dining ticket holders.

Can I change names on my tickets?

Name change must be made in person at the Union Office. The office is open from 9:30am to 4:00pm on weekdays.

You may change names on tickets until 9pm on Saturday 31st January for £10 per name change.

Name changes will cost £20 per name change from then until 9pm on Tuesday 10th February.

Thereafter name changes will cost £30 per name change and can be made up until 11pm on Tuesday 17th February.

Changes will not be reflected on actual tickets but on our electronic records.

I have a problem with my order, what should I do?

Don’t worry! Just send an email to ticketing@2015ball.cus.org and we will sort it out for you.

When is ticket collection?

Ticket collection will take place in January. Union members must bring their membership cards to collect their tickets. Non-members must either bring their University card or some other valid photo ID.
